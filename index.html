My Story










Every deployment begins with a single command.

Mine started on

31st July 2023.

I entered Intellipaat with absolutely no idea about UI/UX.

I didn't know that this one opportunity would completely change my career.










UI/UX became my first classroom.

Soon afterwards,

DevOps became my second home.

The people around me transformed from colleagues into mentors,

friends and family.










Over these three years,

I learned much more than technologies.

I learned ownership.

Leadership.

Communication.

Patience.

Teaching.

Handling customers.

Managing teams.

Solving escalations.

Building confidence.

Building myself.










Whatever I am today,

a huge part of it belongs to



Intellipaat.






















Deployment Timeline










2023




Technical Research Analyst






Started my professional journey at Intellipaat as a Technical Research Analyst, where I discovered my passion for UI/UX Design. Designed user experiences, contributed to course revamps, created presentation assets, and developed engaging learning content. This phase laid the creative and professional foundation that shaped everything that followed.









Late 2023




Curiosity Beyond My Role







I believed growth begins where comfort ends. Beyond my daily responsibilities, I started exploring Linux, strengthening my technical fundamentals while developing the mindset of continuous learning. This was the first step that gradually shifted my journey from design towards Cloud and DevOps.









2024




From Design to Cloud






As my curiosity grew, I immersed myself in AWS, Frontend Development, Python, and CBAP, expanding beyond UI/UX into multiple technical domains. Every new skill wasn't just another certification it was another step toward becoming a more versatile professional.









2024–2025




Senior Research Analyst






Promoted to Senior Research Analyst, I took ownership of both UI/UX and DevOps programs while managing PRTs, learner support, operations, batch planning, stakeholder coordination, and technical escalations. This phase taught me that leadership begins with ownership, long before receiving a leadership title.





    


2025




Engineering the Future






Technology became more than something I learned it became something I lived every day. I deepened my expertise in AWS, DevOps, and DevSecOps, focusing on automation, cloud infrastructure, security, and modern engineering practices while continuing to support large-scale learning programs.





        


2025–2026




Team Lead






Stepping into the role of Team Lead marked the beginning of a new chapter. I had the privilege of mentoring incredible teammates, leading cross-functional initiatives, improving operational excellence, and building an environment where collaboration, ownership, and continuous learning became part of our culture. Looking back, the biggest achievement wasn't the title it was the people, relationships, and memories built along the way.
